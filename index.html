Frequently Asked Questions

Simply contact us by phone, email, or through our Instagram @95autoclub to check availability and book your vehicle. We recommend reserving in advance to guarantee your preferred car. A deposit is required to confirm your booking.

We offer flexible rental periods including daily, weekly, and monthly rates. Whether you need an exotic car for a single day or a luxury vehicle for a longer trip, we have options to fit your schedule and budget.

Renters must be at least 25 years old, hold a valid driver's license, and provide a major credit card for the security deposit. A copy of your insurance or proof of coverage may also be required depending on the vehicle.

Basic insurance coverage is available. We strongly recommend comprehensive coverage for exotic and premium vehicles. Our team will walk you through all coverage options at the time of booking so you can drive with complete peace of mind.

You can reach us by phone at +1 (818) 800-0000, by email at info@95autoclub.com, or visit our website at 95autoclub.com. You can also DM us on Instagram @95autoclub.
